List of Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup Cocoa Powder
  • 1 Cup Whole Wheat Flour
  • 1/2 tsp Baking Soda
  • 1/4 tsp Baking Powder
  • 1/8 tsp Salt
  • 1/2 Cup Granulated Sugar
  • 1/4 Cup applesauce

Directions:

Prep Work: Grate Zucchini and pre-heat oven to 350˚F


In a medium bowl, mix the cocoa powder, flour, baking soda, baking powder, and salt. (Dry Ingredients) In a large bowl, mix the sugar, applesauce, yogurt, zucchini, and egg. (Wet Ingredients) Pour the dry mixture into the wet mixture, and stir until combined. Then, stir in the chocolate chips Spray an 8 X 8 baking dish with cooking spray, then pour the batter into the dish, and bake for 25 – 35 mins (depending on your oven), or when a toothpick/fork inserted in the center comes out clean. Let cool, then cut into squares with sharp knife. Then enjoy!!!
